MHC Regulation in Cancer

Using advanced deep learning on single-cell data to decode cell-type specific regulatory mechanisms of MHC expression in benign and tumor cells, and its involvement in clinical outcomes for immunotherapy.

Deep Learning (PyTorch) Single-Cell RNA-seq Transcriptional Regulation Immunoinformatics

Developed a user-friendly R Shiny web application for exploring single-cell RNA-seq datasets that contain immune checkpoint blockade (ICB) treatment information, making complex data accessible to a broader scientific audience.

TISON: Theatre for in silico Systems Oncology

Led development of a next-generation, web-based, "zero-code" multi-scale modeling and simulation platform for oncology. TISON seamlessly couples information from biomolecular networks to organoid geometries.
